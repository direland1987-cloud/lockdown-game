## Suno Pro Tips

- Use **Instrumental** mode (no vocals)
- Set the duration manually if Suno lets you
- For loopable tracks, listen to the end — it should fade or transition smoothly back to the start
- If a track is close but not quite right, use **Extend** to regenerate just the ending
- For the short jingles (victory/defeat/splash/sting), you may need to trim in Suno or just let it be short
- You can regenerate variations until you find one with the right energy
