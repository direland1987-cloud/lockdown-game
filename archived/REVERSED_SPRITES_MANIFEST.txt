This script generates horizontally-flipped (mirrored) versions of all BJJ grappling
sprites. This is a classic fighting game technique - when a character needs to be on
the opposite side of the screen, you simply use the mirrored sprite instead of
creating entirely new artwork.

from the other direction without needing duplicate artwork.
